Become a volunteer

Volunteer Recruitment Notes

1. Scope of volunteer service


The scope of voluntary services of the Association includes providing services and assistance for the elderly and children, assisting the disabled and needy, assisting medical students, community service, public service, emergency rescue and daily charity activities carried out by the Association and its members.

2. What volunteers need


（一）At least 18 years of age, with full capacity for civil conduct;

（二）Enthusiastic charity and public welfare undertakings, with "dedication, friendship, mutual help, progress" volunteer service spirit;

（三）To perform voluntary services;

（四）Have the basic ability and physical fitness to participate in volunteer services;

（五）Good conduct and abide by national laws and regulations and relevant provisions of volunteer organizations.


3.How do I apply to be a volunteer


（一）Individuals with ID card or student ID card and other valid documents to fill in the "Volunteer Application form" and send to the E-mail address of the association (Link at the end)

（二）Groups are required to obtain the Volunteer Application Form, fill it out and submit it to the email address of the group

（三）After the audit, you can view the change of Personal information after it is successful


4.The obligations that need to be fulfilled after applying to become a volunteer


（一）Comply with national laws and regulations and relevant provisions of volunteer organizations

（二）Provide true, accurate and complete registration information, if there is any change in the information contact to modify.。

（三）Fulfill volunteer service commitments or obligations agreed upon in agreements and complete volunteer service.

（四）Consciously safeguard my image and reputation and that of myself

（五）Consciously safeguard the legitimate rights and interests of those we serve.

（六）When withdrawing from voluntary service activities, perform the obligation of giving reasonable notice.

（七）Keep personal privacy, trade secrets or other legally protected information obtained in the course of participating in voluntary service activities./p>

（八）It is not allowed to solicit remuneration from organizations or individuals accepting voluntary services, or to solicit remuneration in disguised form.。

（九）They are not allowed to engage in any activities for profit or against social morality as volunteers.

（十）Other obligations to be performed according to law.
