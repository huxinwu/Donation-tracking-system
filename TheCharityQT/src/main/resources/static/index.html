Nanosomia Care activities go to poor villages to popularize nanosomia knowledge

AI YOU FOUNDATION

A series of public welfare activities for children's growth and development were launched in poor villages. The event was co-sponsored by the Red Cross Foundation of China and the Second Affiliated Hospital of XX University. The activity invited experts from the Department of Pediatrics and child care of the hospital to give on-site lectures, and carried out a free clinic for children's growth and development, conducted bone age assessment for children with short height, and helped children develop personalized scientific growth programs.
